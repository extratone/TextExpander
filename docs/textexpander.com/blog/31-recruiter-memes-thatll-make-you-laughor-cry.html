    31 Recruiter Memes That’ll Make You Laugh…or Cry

    Maisha CannonPublished December 23, 2019January 6, 2021

    A day in the life of a modern Recruiter includes everything from reviewing resumes and attending meetings, to chasing hiring managers and extending offers. 

    Despite a Recruiter’s best laid plans, however, there are bound to be mishaps – dare we say glitches in the matrix – that can swiftly throw a well-planned day into chaos.

    When you need a momentary respite from the daily hustle, why not indulge in a quick laugh?

    Alison Beard notes, “laughter relieves stress and boredom, boosts engagement and well-being.” In addition, Beard continues, “laughter spurs creativity, collaboration, analytical precision and productivity.”

    With stress relief in mind, we’ve scoured the web for the most humorous Recruiter memes that capture the simple joys and pains of recruiting.

    Enjoy this specially curated collection of 31 Recruiter memes that highlight the uniquely universal moments shared by recruiting professionals across industry, region and company. 

    Recruiter Memes About Candidates

    Candidates – gotta love them in all of their various iterations. From the unprepared to the self-promoting, the jack of all trades to elusive purple squirrel; those who show up half an hour early for interviews to those that cancel 10 minutes before their onsite interview.

    Recruiter Memes About Interviewing

    As Smart Recruiters explains, the hiring process is the process of reviewing applications, selecting the right candidates to interview, testing candidates, choosing between candidates to make the hiring decision and performing various pre-employment tests and checks.

    When the hiring process works, it’s a thing of beauty. But when it doesn’t, it creates an undeniable pain for both recruiters and candidates alike.

    The moment you pick up the phone to extend an offer to the final candidate is a special moment, no many how times you’ve experienced it. Still, those outside of our field may be surprised to learn how many times we hear no, even at this critical juncture. Grab your tissue box in case these memes trigger less than pleasant memories.

    Recruiter Memes About Recruiter Wins/Fails

    As we explore the many recruiting blunders made by our candidates and hiring managers, we must stop and acknowledge the missteps we experience in a typical day in the life.

    Whether we’re moving too fast and forget to make a scheduled call, or we fail to follow up with a hiring manager or candidate when promised, it’s important to recognize our errors, make amends and keep moving. 

    In the words of Payne Stewart, if you can’t laugh at yourself, then how can you laugh at anybody else?

    We love to hate them and hate to love them. But in truth, we’re just BFFs in the making. In the hiring process, the hiring manager is the individual who will ultimately be the direct manager of the new hire. Learning to become strategic partners is a journey. Some days, recruiters have to laugh to keep from crying.

    Recruiter Memes About Sourcing

    Finding people is so easy, said no Sourcer, ever. 

    The process of “identifying, researching, generating, and networking with potential job candidates in order to convert individuals into job applicants” is no easy feat. 

    But what could go wrong, you ask? Messaging mishaps, phone call faux pas and slightly stalkerish behavior can make it hard to connect with the passive prospects we desire most.

    But don’t take our word for it. Check out these sourcing memes that drive home our point.

    Did these recruiter memes make you smile as you face the frustrations that come with recruiting life? 

    Feel free to share these recruiter memes with your recruiting teammates and peers so they too will know that the community understands how they feel.
